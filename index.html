About Me

I have worked at HubSpot since October 2015, currently as a Website Technical Consultant on their Website Migrations team. In my role, I work with HTML/CSS/JS and HubL (a Jinja-based language specific to HubSpot.) I also provide customers with DNS guidance during their website migration. In my spare time, I have become proficient in Python and REST API usage. Using Google Cloud Platform to host my Python applications, I have been able to permanently solve a number of business challenges that were faced by my team at HubSpot.


Inspired by my experience thus far, I have enrolled in the UNH Coding Bootcamp to further my web development learning. Here are the primary goals I have for the course:

  • Develop code management best practices using Git/GitHub.
  • Learn the ins and outs of hosting web and mobile apps.
  • Expanded JavaScript tool set.
  • Connect with Seacoast-area people in the tech industry or in technical roles.

My short term career goal is to move to a role that allows me to write more code, especially JavaScript.

I lives in Kittery, Maine with my wife, son, and dog. When I'm able to break away from work and family, I like to surf the southern ME/NH seacoast, or do something in the mountains (hike, climb, ski, etc.)
