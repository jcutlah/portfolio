About James Cutler

James Cutler has worked for 3+ years at HubSpot, currently as a Website Technical Consultant on their Website Migrations team. In his role, he works with HTML/CSS/JS and HubL (a Jinja-based language specific to HubSpot.) He is also relied upon to provide DNS guidance during the website migration. In his spare time, he has become proficient in Python and REST API usage. Using Google Cloud Platform to host his Python applications, he has been able to permanently solve a number of business challenges that were faced by his team at HubSpot.

Inspired by his experience thus far, James has enrolled in the UNH Coding Bootcamp to further his web development learning. Here are the primary goals he has for the course:

  • Develop code management best practices using Git/GitHub.
  • Learn the ins and outs of hosting web and mobile apps.
  • Expanded JavaScript tool set.
  • Connect with Seacoast-area people in the tech industry or in technical roles.

His short term career goal is to move to a role that allows him to write more code, especially JavaScript.

James lives in Kittery, Maine with his wife, Katie, their son, Elwyn, and their dog, Toby. When he's able to break away from work and family, he likes to surf the southern ME/NH seacoast, or do something in the mountains (hike, climb, ski, etc.)
